FAQ

Q: How do I get paid?

Payment is arranged directly with the buyer through messaging. You can choose cash in-person or an online service like Zelle or CashApp. You keep 93% of your earnings.

Q: Where do I meet buyers?

Meetups happen on the UNT campus at safe, public locations such as Willis Library, the Union, or Eagle Landing.

Q: Can I edit my listing after posting?

Yes! Go to "My Listings" tab to edit, delete, or mark your item as sold at any time.
